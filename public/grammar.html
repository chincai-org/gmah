Grammar Lesson

Topic: Present Simple Tense

The Present Simple tense is used for habits, routines, and general facts.

Examples:
- I play football every weekend.
- She works in an office.
- The sun rises in the east.

Quick Quiz

1. Choose the correct answer:

She plays the piano.
She play the piano.
She playing the piano.
She played the piano every day.

2. Choose the correct answer:

They plays football every weekend.
They play football every weekend.
They playing football every weekend.
They played football every weekend.

3. Choose the correct answer:

The sun rises in the east.
The sun rise in the east.
The sun rising in the east.
The sun rose in the east.

4. Choose the correct answer:

She is cooking dinner now.
He goes to the gym every morning.
They will go to the party tonight.
We visited the museum yesterday.
